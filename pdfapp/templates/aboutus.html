About Us

Welcome to our AI-Powered PDF Question Answering System!

Our platform allows users to upload PDF documents and ask natural language questions to extract accurate answers from the content. Using cutting-edge technology from OpenAI and a robust Django backend, we are making document querying simple and effective.

🧠 Our Mission

To make knowledge extraction from PDFs easier, smarter, and more accessible using modern artificial intelligence.

⚙️ Key Features

  • 🔍 Upload PDF files and ask questions in plain English
  • 🤖 Powered by OpenAI’s advanced language models
  • 🧾 Fast and accurate answers from documents
  • 🔐 Secure and temporary file processing
  • 💻 Clean and user-friendly web interface

👨‍💻 Who We Are

We are students/developers working on this innovative AI solution as part of a summer internship and educational exploration of real-world applications of LLMs and web technologies.
